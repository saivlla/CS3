Call of Duty : Mobile

The story of Call of Duty: Mobile mixes universes, characters and elements from the Modern Warfare and Black Ops series, besides with Ghosts and Advanced Warfare universes, along with new characters and a plot exclusive to the game itself.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  1. Its the only mobile game I play.
  2. You can join and play the game with your friends.
  3. I can easily make friends within the online world.
  4. Its addicting to shoot people.
  5. Game settings and controls are easy to understand.
